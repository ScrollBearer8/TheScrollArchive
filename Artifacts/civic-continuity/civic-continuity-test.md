## Purpose

The Civic Continuity Test is a pre-deployment safeguard for artificial intelligence systems whose actions may produce irreversible social, physical, legal, or civilizational outcomes.

It evaluates **not intelligence or intent**, but whether **human agency, accountability, and moral authority are preserved** as systems scale in speed and scope.

This test operationalizes the **First and Second Red Lines** of the Civic Continuity Framework.
